About Me

During the spring of 2017, I was fortunate enough to visit Japan, this time as a tourist. I had visited Japan many times before but mostly for business. This time, it was all about site seeing, eating good food and experiencing the Japanese culture at its best.

My family and I stayed for about a week. We really enjoyed seeing all the pink cherry blossoms (sakura) blooming at the parks. What we really didn't enjoy were the high prices of the food and souvenirs - boy do things cost a lot in Japan! Overall it was a great trip and I'd love to do it again soon.
